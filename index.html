Why Use Spendora & Quick Start Guide
Why Use Spendora?
  • Privacy-First: All data stays on your device - no cloud storage
  • Visual Insights: See your spending patterns with interactive charts
  • Multi-Currency: Support for 11+ major world currencies
  • Export Ready: Download summaries and backup your data anytime
  • Mobile Friendly: Works perfectly on all devices
Quick Start Guide
  1. 1. Add Your First Transaction Use the form on the left to record income or expenses
  2. 2. Choose Categories Organize transactions by type (Food, Transport, Salary, etc.)
  3. 3. View Your Budget Charts update automatically to show spending patterns
  4. 4. Track & Export Monitor your balance and download reports as needed
